More InDepth about the robot architecture

This is a 2DOF SCARA (Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm), which from its name is a robot designed specifically for assembly purposes on the X/Y plane. Normally, the industrial robot comes with 4DOF. Which are the Z axis and the rotating axis around Z to enable a better orientation of the handled objects.
